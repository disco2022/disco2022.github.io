Participation in this workshop is by invitation only.

If you have been contacted by the organizers, click [HERE] to register.

First you need to choose between accommodation in a single room (€650) or a double room (€575); accommodation is provided on the wonderful premises of Bertinoro Castle. Please also mark the checkbox to tell us if you want to participate in the excursion to Ravenna on the afternoon of Wednesday July 22.

Next you need to fill in your personal details and select the method of payment. (Leave the "fiscal number" blank if you are not an Italian citizen.)

The registration fee is all-inclusive: it covers all local expenses, including meals, coffee breaks, and accomodation for 5 nights (from arrival Sunday afternoon through departure on Friday afternoon).
